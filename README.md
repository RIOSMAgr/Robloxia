# Robloxia - Roblox Mod Menu Injector

Robloxia is a powerful modding tool for Roblox that comes pre-loaded with a variety of mods, including features like enhanced run speed and jump height, which work in most servers.

## Pre-Loaded Mods

Robloxia includes a selection of pre-loaded mods that enhance your Roblox experience:

- **Run Speed Enhancement**: Increase your character's running speed for faster movement in the game.
- **Jump Height Modification**: Adjust your character's jump height to reach new heights and access hidden areas.
- **Infinite Health**: Never worry about running out of health in-game.
- **Fly Mode**: Enable or disable flying capabilities for your character.
- **Teleportation**: Teleport to specific locations on the map.
- **No-Clip Mode**: Move through walls and obstacles as if they don't exist.
- **Invisibility**: Become invisible to other players.
- **Custom Controls**: Remap keys and controls to suit your preferences.

## How it Works

Robloxia works by injecting custom code into the DLLs of Roblox during runtime. This enables you to modify the game's behavior and access additional features, including the pre-loaded mods. Here's a simplified explanation of the process:

1. **Start Roblox**: Launch Roblox as you normally would.

2. **Run Robloxia**: Execute the Robloxia tool by double-clicking on `Robloxia.bat`.

3. **Inject Code**: Robloxia will inject custom Lua scripts into the Roblox process, enabling the mod menu and activating the pre-loaded mods.

4. **Use the Mod Menu**: Access the mod menu in Roblox to enable or disable various mods and features.
